About Stephanie

My Approach

Solution-Focused Therapy allows me to celebrate people’s identity, strength, resourcefulness, determination, hope, and faith.

Many clients come to therapy because they are traumatized (PTSD). Rather than reliving the trauma you have experienced, I will ask questions to help you recognize your own unique qualities and skills. My job is to help you see yourself as you really are so that you can become the best possible you – the person you are truly born to be.

We will move forward at your own pace, in the areas that you choose to focus on.

Couples Therapy

Are you ready to experience a positive change in your relationship? My role is to help you and your partner to identify what you want your relationship to look like. We then work to identify steps that can be taken for your relationship to once again flourish.

When things are going well, we forget to notice why and what we are doing to cause things to be so right. Together we’ll explore what you have done in the past and what you can do now to bring back your love and joy.

My solution-focused therapy approach helps you learn what your skills are and how to move forward into the future with hope.

LGBTQ+ Therapy

I have the privilege of walking with LGBT+ individuals who are in the process of discovering their true identities. I provide a safe space to explore who you are, and with my solution focused approach, I will help you focus on your desired hopes and dreams.
